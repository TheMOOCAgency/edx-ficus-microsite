    About MOOC Conservation

    MOOC Conservation is a platform that is dedicated to online courses on nature conservation. The training materials are developed by IUCN-PAPACO (the International Union for Conservation of Nature’s Programme on African Protected Areas and Conservation) and its partners. The courses are free of charge and open to all.
