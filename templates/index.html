What is a MOOC ?

A Massive Open Online Course, or MOOC, is an interactive, collaborative web-based course format designed to encourage participation and achievement.

What is Adaptive Learning ?

Adaptive learning is an online educational system in which the sequencing and content of the course depends on student performance. Regular feedback from the course platform allows for greater personalization of learning paths, higher levels of engagement, and more effective memorization.
