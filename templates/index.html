A TRILOGY OF MOOC ON HERMORRHAGE MANAGEMENT

Scrollez pour découvrir le contenu de la formation

POST-PARTUM

Worldwide, 1 woman is dying during every 4 minutes in labour mostly due to uncontrolled bleeding in both developed and less developed countries.
Let’s see together the different risks factors of post-partum haemorrhage and how to monitor easily the bleeding.
Also, depending on the resources available, let’s see how to be ready to take in charge these difficult situations.

TRAUMATOLOGY

Up to 40% of deaths after trauma are attributable to uncon trolled bleeding and its consequences.
This makes bleeding the most common potentially preventable cause of death following injury.
Let’s see how to manage trauma to prevent as much as possible uncontrolled bleeding.

CARDIAC SURGERY

Up to 10% of cardiac surgery patients experience major bleeding either during their surgery or in the immediate post-operative period, putting these patients in danger.
Prevention, detection and management of these patients is therefore crucial, let’s see how to care for these patients.
